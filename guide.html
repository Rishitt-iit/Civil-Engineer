Step-by-Step Construction Guide

1. Site Clearing

Remove vegetation, debris and unwanted materials before excavation.

2. Excavation

Dig foundation trenches according to structural drawings.

3. Foundation Work

PCC, footing reinforcement and concrete pouring process.

4. Column & Beam Casting

Install reinforcement and shuttering before concrete placement.

5. Brickwork

Wall construction with alignment and curing checks.

6. Finishing Work

Plaster, putty, paint, flooring and electrical fittings.
